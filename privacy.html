Who operates this site

This static portfolio is published by Lenilson Pinheiro Valério. Contact: lenilsonpinheiro@gmail.com.

Contact form

When you use the contact form on the portfolio, you voluntarily provide your name, email address, and message. This information is submitted over HTTPS to a Google Apps Script endpoint (Web App) and processed with Google’s MailApp service to deliver an email to the portfolio owner. Google processes this request under Google’s own terms and policies.

The form includes a hidden honeypot field to reduce automated spam; if it is filled, the submission is discarded.

Purpose and retention

Data is used solely to read and reply to your inquiry. Messages are handled like normal email in the owner’s mailbox. There is no sale of personal data and no marketing lists are built from the form.

Third parties

Hosting: GitHub Pages. Form backend: Google Apps Script / Google. No other subprocessors are used for the form beyond those required to load the page (e.g. fonts or CDNs referenced in the portfolio).

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g. LGPD / GDPR), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. To exercise these rights, email lenilsonpinheiro@gmail.com.
